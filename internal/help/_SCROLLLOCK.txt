{{DISPLAYTITLE: _SCROLLLOCK}}
The [[_SCROLLLOCK]] statement sets the state of the Scroll Lock key.


{{PageSyntax}}
: [[_SCROLLLOCK]] {ON|OFF|_TOGGLE}
