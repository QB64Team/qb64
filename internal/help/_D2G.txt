{{DISPLAYTITLE:_D2G}}
The [[_D2G]] function converts a '''degree''' value into a '''gradient''' value. 


{{PageSyntax}}
: {{Parameter|result}} = [[_D2G]]({{Parameter|num}})


==Availability==
* '''Version 1.000 and up.'''


{{PageExamples}}
''Example:'' Coverting Degrees into Gradient.
{{CodeStart}}
INPUT &quot;Give me an angle in Degrees &quot;, D
R = _D2G(D)
PRINT &quot;That angle in Gradient is &quot;; R
{{CodeEnd}}
{{OutputStart}}
Give me an angle in Degrees 60
That angle in Gradient is  66.66666
{{OutputEnd}}
